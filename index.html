About Me

Crafting visuals frame by frame

Sahil Suman Senapati Motion Designer & Visual Artist · AAFT

A motion designer and video editor with a passion for creating cinematic experiences that go beyond the screen.

Trained in the full Adobe Creative Suite — After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Dreamweaver — I bring ideas to life through motion, light, and design.
